### Persona: Visitor
**Who they are:** A first-time, anonymous person evaluating whether this platform is worth using, usually as someone looking for a PG or an owner considering whether to list one.
**What they want:** They want quick proof that the platform can help them without demanding commitment up front. At this stage, they are trying to understand whether the listings feel relevant, whether the search experience fits how they think about location, and whether the marketplace looks active and trustworthy enough to justify registration. Their goal is not deep usage yet; it is confidence that this is a useful starting point.
**What they fear:** They fear wasting time on a platform that looks promising but turns out to be thin, confusing, outdated, or irrelevant to their city or needs. If the preview feels weak, they assume the full experience will not be better.
**How they relate:** They sit at the top of the funnel and may become either a User or an Owner, depending on why they arrived.

### Persona: User
**Who they are:** A student or similar accommodation seeker trying to find a suitable PG near a preferred area or in a specific city without visiting many places blindly.
**What they want:** They want a faster, simpler path from need to shortlist. Their main outcome is to discover nearby or city-based PG options, compare them with enough confidence to narrow choices, and reach out to the right owner without unnecessary back-and-forth. The platform matters to them because it should reduce effort, save time, and make the search feel manageable instead of chaotic or fragmented across many offline visits and informal leads.
**What they fear:** They fear missing better options, contacting the wrong places, or relying on outdated information that leads to wasted travel, delayed decisions, or settling for a poor fit.
**How they relate:** They depend on Owners to keep listings accurate and available, and on Admins to maintain a trustworthy marketplace; many begin as Visitors before registering.

### Persona: Owner
**Who they are:** A PG owner or operator who needs a reliable way to present their property, stay visible, and attract interested renters without depending only on offline discovery.
**What they want:** Their primary goal is steady, real demand. They want the platform to turn their PG into something discoverable by the right people, help them keep its information current with minimal friction, and produce consistent inquiries that can become actual occupants. For them, success is not just being listed; it is being found by serious seekers and feeling that the platform creates meaningful business value rather than another channel to maintain for little return.
**What they fear:** They fear low visibility, weak or inconsistent inquiry flow, and the sense that effort spent maintaining listings does not translate into credible leads. They also fear stale information damaging trust.
**How they relate:** They rely on Users for demand, on Visitors as future demand, and on Admins to keep the marketplace fair, credible, and active.

### Persona: Admin
**Who they are:** A platform administrator or operations stakeholder responsible for keeping the PG marketplace trustworthy, balanced, and healthy as it grows.
**What they want:** They want a marketplace where supply and demand both grow, listings stay active, interactions between users and owners are meaningful, and the overall experience feels credible enough to sustain repeat usage. Their outcome is a well-run ecosystem, not just raw signups: they need quality participation, healthy engagement, and evidence that the platform is producing successful connections between seekers and owners.
**What they fear:** They fear a marketplace that fills with poor-quality or inappropriate listings, loses trust, grows unevenly, or fails to convert traffic into useful activity. For them, failure looks like churn, weak engagement, and a platform that no longer feels dependable.
**How they relate:** They support and govern the relationship between Visitors, Users, and Owners by monitoring activity, moderating listings, and protecting overall marketplace quality.
