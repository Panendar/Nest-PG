## Page / Screen Copy

### 1. User Home / Search

- Headline: Find the right PG near you
- Subheading: Search by city or nearby radius, compare options, and contact owners directly.
- Body text: Start with a location to see PGs that match your needs.

### 2. Search Results

- Headline: PG options for your search
- Subheading: Review available listings and open details to compare better.
- Body text: Update city or radius anytime to refine results.

### 3. Listing Details

- Headline: PG details
- Subheading: Check key information and availability before contacting the owner.
- Body text: Compare this option with others if you are still deciding.

### 4. Compare Listings

- Headline: Compare shortlisted PGs
- Subheading: See your selected options side by side to choose confidently.
- Body text: When ready, contact the owner of your preferred listing.

### 5. Contact Owner

- Headline: Send inquiry to owner
- Subheading: Share your interest and preferred move-in details.
- Body text: Your inquiry will be sent directly to the listing owner.

## Form Labels & Placeholders

### Search Form

- Label: City
- Placeholder: Example: Hyderabad
- Label: Search radius
- Placeholder: Select distance from your current location

### Contact Form

- Label: Full name
- Placeholder: Enter your full name
- Label: Phone number
- Placeholder: Enter a reachable 10-digit phone number
- Label: Preferred move-in date
- Placeholder: Select your expected move-in date
- Label: Message to owner
- Placeholder: Example: I am interested in this PG for next month. Please share visit timings.

## Empty States

### Search Results Empty

- Message: No PG listings found for this location yet.
- Supporting text: Try a different city or increase your search radius.
- CTA: Try Another Search

### Compare List Empty

- Message: You have not selected listings to compare.
- Supporting text: Add at least two listings from search results.
- CTA: Back to Results

## Error Messages

- City is required: Please enter a city to continue.
- Radius missing: Please select a search radius.
- Invalid phone number: Enter a valid phone number so the owner can reach you.
- Contact message too short: Please add a few details so the owner can respond meaningfully.
- Network issue on search: We could not load listings right now. Please try again.
- Network issue on inquiry: Your inquiry could not be sent. Please check your connection and retry.
- Permission/authentication: Please sign in to view full details and contact owners.

## Success Messages

- Search applied: Search updated. Showing PGs for your latest location.
- Inquiry sent: Your inquiry was sent to the owner successfully.
- Compare ready: Listings added. You can now compare your selected options.
